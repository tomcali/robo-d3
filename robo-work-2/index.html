Investment Objective: Capital Preservation

With capital preservation as your investment objectiive, most of your assets should be in cash, bonds, and stocks managed by Strategic Robo Investments. You can redistribute these assets by using the buttons above to increase or decrease percentages invested across five classes of assets. Adding to an asset class will decrease cash.
